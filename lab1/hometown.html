Welcome to Abuja!

City Information

  • Country: Nigeria
  • State: Federal Capital Territory (FCT)
  • Population: 3,067,500
  • Minister of FCT: Mohammed Musa Bello

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, located in the North Central - sometimes referred to as the Middle-Belt of the country.

The city houses the Federal government of the country, as well as many corporate headquarters and government parastatals.

The city is naturally beautified by hills and rocks, including the well-known Zuma Rock.

Major Sports Teams

  1. Super Eagles
  2. Abuja F.C.
  3. NAF Rockets F.C
